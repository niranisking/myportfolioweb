projects


Soccer News Mailing App

The Soccer News Mailing App helps get information (name, email) of users interested in getting trending soccers news update via their mails, and posts this information to an external resource ( Mailchimp ), where the content is put together and delivered to subscribers. This was achieved by consuming the API of the Mailchimp server


Weather Report App

The Weather Report App helps users stay up to date with the weather condition at different locations.This was achieved by simply consuming the API of popular weather report site OpenWeatherMap. The idea is to simply input the location into a form field, click on submit, and boom! details on the weather condition of that location is displayed on the screen


Fruit Recipes Blog

The Fruit recipes blog is aimed at equipping its audience with great information on the health benefits of several fruit recipes. Are you a Nutritionist? I’ve made sure that for every fruit recipe which is of interest to you, a link with detailed information on its preparation steps, cook time etc. can be found. Check this out!

The idea behind the SCRAMBLE TEXT APP project is to help Users safely share content by scrambling portions of the text they don’t want the world to read/see. A user is to write/paste some content into a form field and then specify the words that must be scrambled (using another field)
